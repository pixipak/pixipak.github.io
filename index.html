Vision

There are currently no fashionable alternatives to Camelbak backpack, refillable bladders typically used by hikers and outdoor enthusiasts. The Pixipak is a fashionable Camelbak that has a disguised mouthpiece and built-in phone charger while additionally offering hidden pockets to protect valuables from thefts that are commonplace at festivals. Additionally, not everyone in the world is fortunate enough to have drinkable water at their disposal. By supporting pixipak, you are joining our revolution to bring clean water to kids in developing countries.

Hydration

Having to wait in water lines with no end insight while your favorite artist is performing is the biggest buzzkill at a festival. We believe staying hydrated and safe is a priority and should go hand in hand with keeping your style fresh.

Charge

Whether you’re stranded on a hike or in the middle of a concert, battery charge is constantly getting drained by taking pictures and trying to connect with friends. With a built in charger, you will never have to deal with a dead phone again.
